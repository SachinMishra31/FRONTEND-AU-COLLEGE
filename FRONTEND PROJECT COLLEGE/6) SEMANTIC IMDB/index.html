    • The incrediable Hulk (2008)

    In The Incredible Hulk (2008), Dr. Bruce Banner becomes the Hulk after a failed experiment with gamma radiation. He changes into the Hulk when he gets angry or his heart rate goes up. While hiding and looking for a cure, Banner has to deal with General Ross, who wants to use the Hulk as a weapon. When a new villain, Emil Blonsky, becomes the powerful Abomination, Banner turns into the Hulk to stop him. After a big fight, Hulk defeats Abomination but doesn’t kill him because Betty asks him not to. In the end, Banner learns to control his power.

    Favourite Character:

    Bruce Banner, played by Edward Norton, is liked for his inner struggle and vulnerability. The Hulk, his powerful and wild side, is also memorable. Hulk’s strength and action contrast with Bruce’s calmness, showing the clash between human emotions and great power.

    Impactful Scenes:

    - The big fight between the Hulk and Abomination in Harlem is one of the movie’s most exciting scenes. Both are very strong, but at the end, Hulk chooses not to kill Abomination. This shows that Hulk has a sense of right and wrong and can make merciful choices even with his great power.
